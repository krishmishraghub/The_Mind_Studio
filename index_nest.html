About Us

NEST Cluster at IIT Guwahati is a dynamic initiative dedicated to nurturing innovation, entrepreneurship, and technological advancement among students and researchers. We provide a collaborative platform for interdisciplinary projects, workshops, and mentorship programs that bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world applications. Through our activities, we aim to cultivate a culture of innovation and help transform ideas into impactful solutions that address contemporary challenges.

Our Activities

Innovation Workshop Series
